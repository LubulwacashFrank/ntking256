INSTRUCTIONS: Update Your .env File with REAL Flutterwave Keys

1. Get your keys from: https://dashboard.flutterwave.com/settings/apis

2. Open your .env file

3. Replace these lines:

   FROM (current fake keys):
   FLW_PUBLIC_KEY=FLWPUBK_TEST-SANDBOXDEMOKEY123456789-X
   FLW_SECRET_KEY=FLWSECK_TEST-SANDBOXDEMOKEY123456789-X
   FLW_ENCRYPTION_KEY=FLWSECK_TEST-SANDBOXDEMOKEY123456789-X

   TO (your real keys from Flutterwave):
   FLW_PUBLIC_KEY=FLWPUBK_TEST-paste-your-real-public-key-here
   FLW_SECRET_KEY=FLWSECK_TEST-paste-your-real-secret-key-here
   FLW_ENCRYPTION_KEY=FLWSECK_TEST-paste-your-real-encryption-key-here

4. Save the .env file

5. Restart your server:
   npm start

6. Test at: http://localhost:3000/auth

7. You should now see "Mobile money payment ready (MTN, Airtel, Vodafone)"

8. Register as farmer with your real phone number and you'll get payment prompt!

---

IMPORTANT: The keys must be REAL keys from Flutterwave dashboard.
Demo/fake keys will NOT work!
